About

Green Harvest Organics is a family-run organic farm cooperative based in Ratnapura, Sri Lanka. We cultivate premium cinnamon, pepper, cloves, and cardamom using traditional methods passed down through generations. Our mission is to provide the purest spices while supporting local farming communities and preserving biodiversity. We employ 45 farmers across 12 farms, all certified under organic PGS standards. Every product is traced from tree to table.
