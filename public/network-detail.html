About this Network

The Organic PGS (Participatory Guarantee System) Sri Lanka is a community-based quality assurance system. It certifies organic producers based on active participation of stakeholders, including farmers, consumers, and agricultural advisors. The system is built on trust, social networks, and knowledge exchange.
